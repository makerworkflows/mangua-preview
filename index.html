Why Mangua?

Not a Gummy.
Not a Leather.
Not a Chip.

Most "fruit snacks" are held together with added sugar, citric acid, and ingredients you can't pronounce. Mangua is different.

We take ripe Mexican mangoes and freeze-dry them into a crunchy, ready-to-eat bar. One ingredient. That's it. No sugar added. No concentrates. No preservatives. No sticky fingers.

The result is a light, crispy bar with the full flavor of fresh mango -- the kind of snack you'd actually feel good putting in a lunchbox.

The Process

From Mango Tree to Your Table

Three steps. No shortcuts. No compromises.

1

Harvested Ripe

Our mangoes are picked at peak sweetness from farms in Mexico -- not harvested early and ripened in a warehouse.

2

Freeze-Dried Fresh

Low-temperature freeze-drying removes moisture while locking in the flavor, color, and nutrients of fresh mango. No heat. No cooking. No nutrient loss.

3

Pressed into a Bar

The freeze-dried mango is formed into a structured, crunchy bar you can eat anywhere -- no bowl, no spoon, no mess.
